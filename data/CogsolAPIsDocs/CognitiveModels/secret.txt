### Secret Model

Represents a confidential key-value pair stored securely, typically for authentication or integration purposes.

---

#### `name` *(string)*  <span style={{ color: 'red', fontWeight: 'bold' }}>Required</span>  
The identifier or label for the secret.  
- **Min length**: 1  
- **Max length**: 127  

---
#### `value` *(string)*  <span style={{ color: 'red', fontWeight: 'bold' }}>Required</span>  
The confidential value of the secret.  
- **Min length**: 1  
- **Max length**: 255  

---

#### Example
```json
{
  "name": "string",
  "value": "string"
}
```
