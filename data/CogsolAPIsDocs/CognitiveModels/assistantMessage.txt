### AssistantMessage Model

Represents a message generated by the assistant during a conversation.

---

#### `message` *(string)* <span style={{ color: 'red', fontWeight: 'bold' }}>Required</span>  
The content of the message sent by the assistant.  

#### Example
```json
{
  "message": "string"
}
```
